## CIE XYZ analytic fit

The continuous-rainbow color conversion reimplements the published analytic
fit coefficients for the CIE 1931 XYZ color-matching functions from:

- Chris Wyman, Peter-Pike Sloan, and Peter Shirley, “Simple Analytic
  Approximations to the CIE XYZ Color Matching Functions,” JCGT 2(2), 2013
- Source: https://jcgt.org/published/0002/02/01/
- Paper license: CC BY-ND 3.0
